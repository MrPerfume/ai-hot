To set it up, you need to download and install the Save to Spotify CLI from GitHub.

Then you just prompt your AI agent as normal, but tack on “and save to Spotify,” and it should show up right in your podcast feed.

Now, we’re making it possible to save and play Personal Podcasts on Spotify.
